📜 Realmbound Wilds Chronicle Guide

🎮 Controls

Mouse: Left click + Drag to pan. Scroll wheel to zoom. Click individual cells to inspect them.

Keyboard: Use W A S D or the arrow keys to pan around. Use + and - to zoom in and out.

🌌 Realm Dimensions

  • 🌳 Overworld: Standard continent layout with oceans, plains, forests, mountains, and trade routes.
  • 🌋 Underworld: Cavern systems filled with solid stone, deep lakes, crystals, and magma vents.
  • ☁️ Aether: Floating sky islands surrounded by starlight woods and storm peaks.
  • ☄️ Space: Dark space orbit hosting asteroid belts, nebulas, and comet tails. Outposts require Life Support Domes!

👑 Factions

  • Valoria: Expansionists (expands settlements 30% faster).
  • Oakhaven: Forest Wardens (forest fire durations halved; faster vegetation growth).
  • Ironclad: Miner Faction (doubled speed of iron/gold mining).
  • Sunspire: Diplomat Faction (alliance formation rate doubled).
